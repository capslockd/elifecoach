Meet Your Coaches

Pastor Jimm & Pynqi Gorgonia

Jimm and Pynqi Gorgonia are life coaches, faith leaders, and parents whose mission is simple: to help you live a life that truly matters.

As a pastoral couple with over a decade of ministry experience, they have walked with hundreds of individuals and families through seasons of transition, identity crisis, relational challenges, and personal breakthrough.

Their coaching is rooted in scripture, shaped by real-world wisdom, and delivered with compassion and accountability. Together, they bring a powerful dynamic — Jimm's pastoral insight and Pynqi's empathetic presence — to every coaching relationship.
